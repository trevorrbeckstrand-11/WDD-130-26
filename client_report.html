Project Reflection Report

Client Report

How did you decide what content and images to use for your site plan?

My site was tennis themed, so I visited a few popular tennis websites for inspiration. I also used colors similar to tennis balls, rackets, and tennis courts.

How did you communicate with your developer? Was there any issues with communication?

I had my developer switched about a week into the project, which made coordinating more difficult. Since Franklin was in a different class, communication took more effort, but we were able to work things out.

Did your developer meet your expectations for your site?

Yes. It looks great to me. I'm excited to see the final product, but so far I'm very happy with it.

What will you do differently next time you hire a team to do a project for you?

I'll communicate more often. We both tried our best, but it was difficult getting in contact with Franklin at times.

Developer Report

How did you communicate with your client? Was there any issues?

We mostly communicated through text messages, but we met once in person, which helped us figure out most of the project. It was sometimes difficult getting images from my client, but everything came together in the end.

How did you handle publishing your client's site and sharing it with the client?

Publishing went smoothly. We simply shared the GitHub Pages links with each other.

How was your experience with managing your site with GitHub?

It was a little confusing at first because I had never used GitHub before, but it became much easier as I gained experience.

General Experience & Reflections

What was the most challenging part of this project?

The most challenging part was creating my own site plan and wireframes. They took the longest, but resources like Coolors helped me develop a color palette.

What did you enjoy most?

Seeing someone else build the website that I had planned.

What would you do differently?

I would communicate more consistently. We were both busy, and better communication would have helped us understand each other's expectations.

Do you feel more confident working on real-world projects?

Yes. This project gave me confidence that I can take a wireframe and turn it into a functional website while making adjustments based on client feedback.
